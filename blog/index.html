Editorial slant

I have decided not to put any limitation concerning the covered disciplines in this blog, as long as I consider myself qualified enough to write about it. I might write opinion-based posts but the premises on which I base my reasoning must remain true regardless of any school of thought. A post might be aimed at the general public or at a smaller subset of readers depending on the technicality and prerequisites that its writing implies.
